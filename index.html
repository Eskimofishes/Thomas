Singapore’s First Data-Led Cafeteria Management Company

Catering Corner Pte Ltd is a new-age cafeteria management company owned by ex-consultants who were tired of bad cafeteria food and missed meals. We realised corporate cafeterias lacked what we knew best—data, structure, and accountability that drive higher performance.

So we built Catering Corner. Using data, we align the needs of employees, employers, and food partners to deliver satisfying meals on time and on budget. As an owner-managed team, we treat every cafeteria like a live project— measured, improved, and personally handled.
